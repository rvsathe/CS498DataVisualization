About and How to Use.

Showcasing the menus present on the NYPL site.. This is an Interactive Slide-show with bubble charts and an area chart.

To navigate your way, please press the buttons in the corner. The highlighted arrows indicate which direction you can move in.

What you see.

  1. The first scene is a chart showing when the menus were collected.
  2. The second shows the most popular dishes.
  3. The third shows the dishes by age (100 years,75 years,less than 75 years)
